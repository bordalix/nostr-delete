Nostr delete

Delete your Nostr notes.

Know more

This app will send kind 5 (aka "delete") events asking relays to delete your notes.

It's not guaranteed that relays will delete your notes.

It requires a browser extension which implements window.nostr (like Alby)

If you want to use your own list of relays:

  • Save this site or clone repo
  • Edit js/relays.js
  • Open index.html with a browser
